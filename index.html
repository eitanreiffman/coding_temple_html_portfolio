About Me

Hey there! My name is Eitan Reiffman, and I was born and raised in Los Angeles, California. I've moved around a lot throughout my life, but I currently live in New York. I love hanging out with my family, playing basketball, trying out new cuisines, traveling, and rewatching Lord of the Rings for the 111th time. I've worked as a writer, editor, campaign manager, and copywriter. I'm also a musician, singer-songwriter, and producer. I love all creative art forms, which is one of the many factors that led me to software programming.

Becoming a Programmer

If you told me a year ago that one year later I'd be studying to be a software developer, I would've found that pretty shocking. But I guess it was always in the back of my mind, and perhaps I just needed the right life circumstance to give me that push. I don't believe in worthwhile results without the proper dedication and hard work. That being said, I think hard work can also be something enjoyable with the right attitude.
